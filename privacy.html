Privacy / Local Save Data

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Vibe The Game stores progress only inside your browser. No analytics, no ads, no remote account, and no gameplay data sent to a server.

What We Store

The game keeps only the information needed to resume your adventure.

Progress Player location, unlocked areas, active quests, bug counts, catches, and achievements.
Collection Data Inventory, discovered bug species, fish catches, pet companion, and combo milestones.
Settings Your local consent choice for whether saving is allowed in the browser.

Where It Lives

Save data is stored locally on your device using browser storage. Nothing is uploaded to us.

Technology Purpose Typical Lifetime
IndexedDB Primary save storage for the full game state. Until you clear site data.
localStorage Fallback copy when IndexedDB is unavailable. Until you clear site data.
Cookies Consent flag and legacy fallback save support. Up to 1 year.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not use tracking cookies.
  • We do not use analytics, ads, or fingerprinting.
  • We do not ask you to create an account.
  • We do not transmit gameplay data to an external service.

Your Controls

You can delete your local progress at any time from the in-game menu or by clearing this site’s browser storage. You can also choose to play without saving.

Children's Privacy

Vibe The Game is designed as a general audience browser game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults because gameplay data remains on the local device.

Changes

If storage behavior changes in the future, this page will be updated with the new storage methods and the revised date above.

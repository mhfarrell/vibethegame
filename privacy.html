Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Overview

Vibe The Game ("we", "the game") is a browser-based game that respects your privacy. We collect minimal data, store everything locally on your device, and never transmit your information to external servers.

Data We Collect

The only data we store is your game progress, which includes:

  • Player position and current area
  • Bug count and inventory items
  • Quest progress and achievements
  • Play time

How We Store Data

All game data is stored locally on your device using:

TechnologyPurposeDuration
CookiesPrimary game save storage1 year
localStorageBackup game save storageUntil cleared

No data is sent to any server. Your game progress exists only in your browser.

Cookies

We use the following cookies:

Cookie NamePurposeType
vibeCookieConsentRemembers your cookie consent choiceFunctional
vibeGameSaveStores your game progressFunctional

We do not use any tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or advertising cookies.

Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties. We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The game runs entirely in your browser with no external dependencies.

Children's Privacy

Vibe The Game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your data at any time by clearing your browser cookies and localStorage, or using the "Delete Save" option in the game menu.
  • Play without cookies — the game works without saving, though progress will be lost when you close the browser.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will change the "Last updated" date at the top. Since no personal data is collected, changes would only relate to new storage mechanisms for game progress.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through the game's repository.
